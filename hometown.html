Massena was established in 1802. It is the home of our Alcoa, which is the world's longest continuously operating aluminum smelter. We have a population of ~12,000 people.

We have Springs Park, which had presidents and royalty coming down for it's belived 'healing properties'. This town is in upstate New, York.
